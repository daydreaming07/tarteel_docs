Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 16, 2026

Tarteel ("the App") is a Quran learning game for Android and iOS. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is handled.

Data We Collect

Tarteel does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking of any kind.

Network Requests

The App connects to third-party Quran APIs solely to download ayah text and recitation audio files. These downloads are cached on your device so the App can function offline after the initial fetch. No personal information is sent in these requests.

On-Device Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Quran ayah text (for display in the game)
  • Recitation audio files (for playback)
  • Your in-app preferences (theme, font, reciter selection)
  • Game progress (current surah and ayah for resume)

This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, crash reporting, or advertising services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us by opening an issue on our GitHub repository.
